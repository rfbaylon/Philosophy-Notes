## Ingredients

Wet
- 3 ripe bananas
- 1 egg
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup wheat germ

Dry
- 2 cups flour
- a little salt
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp baking powder

## Directions

1. Mash bananas. Add egg, sugar, wheat germ.
2. Mix dry ingredients
3. Add dry to wet
4. Pour in pan, bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes to an hour.
