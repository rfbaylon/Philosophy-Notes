- Maunakea visitor center after dark
- Akaka Falls
- Hawaii Tropical Bioreserve
- Punalu'u or Pololu black sand beaches
- Nighttime manta ray diving
- Kona
- Hilo & Hamakua Coast
- Puʻuhonua o Honaunau & Honaunau Bay snorkeling
- Hapuna beach (relax)
