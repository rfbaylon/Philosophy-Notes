
## Surrogacy
Start to finish, surrogacy takes about 14 months
Expect to pay 150k-220k

[https://gwkacademy.org/gay-surrogacy-costs/](https://gwkacademy.org/gay-surrogacy-costs/)

International surrogacy is a pretty popular option, and is a lot cheaper. Surrogacy in other states is also likely cheaper. Mexico, Canada, and Colombia seem to be popular options.
  
Often, surrogacy is covered by insurance. Its worth checking employee benefits to see what’s covered.

## Adoption

Costs between 2-3k

about 2 year wait time

There are chances employers cover fees for adoption in some capacity as part of a benefits package.

Mass pays for tuition for adoptees to go to state schools (though reddit tells me this is difficult to access atm)

With adoption there are more logistical and legal barriers. There is less legal security that you will get the adopted child you want, and for adoption, you need to take courses, have home inspections, etc. to be approved.
